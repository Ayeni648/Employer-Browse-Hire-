About this demo

  • This is a front-end-only simulation of the employer Browse → Hire → Pay flow. There is no Laravel/MySQL backend behind this page — all data lives in memory in your browser tab.
  • Refreshing or closing the tab clears any hires you've created. Nothing is sent anywhere.
  • "Payment" is simulated: confirming just flips a status flag from pending to paid. No payment gateway is contacted.
  • Engagement amounts are estimated as hourly rate × estimated hours for the selected duration (assuming roughly 20 hrs/week).
  • All freelancer names, bios, and project history are invented placeholder data.
